HISTORY

Mona Cakes has been serving traditional European cakes in Ipoh, Perak since 2003.

Founded by a Spanish émigré, the bakery is known for its classic continental recipes, warm service, and old-world charm.

A local favourite for coffee and cake, Mona Cakes is where tradition meets indulgence. Every cake is baked with heritage, passion, and heart.
